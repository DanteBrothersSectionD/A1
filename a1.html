Our Top Researcher

Dr. Jack Bright rose to the top of his field in bioengineering and abnormal genetics. Bright was recruited by the Foundation and was given SCP-963 to work with. Dr. Bright died as a result of a containment breach of SCP-076-2, but his consciousness was attached to SCP-963 in the process and has worked with several SCPs.
